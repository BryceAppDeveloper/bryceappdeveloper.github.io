Privacy

MorForm apps collect no personal data and run no servers — there's no MorForm account. Your data stays on your device unless you choose to send it: by email, or by syncing through a shared cloud folder you connect (your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive). For full details see the privacy policies below.
