Why not a spreadsheet?

Old way vs. MorForm

Construction sites have run on paper, email threads, and spreadsheets for decades. MorForm puts it all in one loop.

The old way

  • Hours tracked on paper, re-entered in Excel at the end of the week
  • Safety forms printed, signed by hand, scanned and emailed as JPGs
  • Job estimates live in a spreadsheet only one person can update
  • Report emails with 10 loose attachments, no standard format
  • Another SaaS tool that keeps your data on someone else's server

MorForm

  • Time logged on site in seconds, exported as a signed PDF in one tap
  • FLHA / JHA filled digitally, and safety PDFs route to the right inbox automatically
  • Estimate builds the dispatch package: no re-entry, no version drift
  • One .morrep file: time, expenses, safety forms, all in one attachment
  • One-time purchase, and no MorForm account required

Common questions

Straight answers

The four questions every crew asks first.

How much does MorForm cost?
MorForm is a one-time purchase. MorForm (Supervisor) is $6.99 USD ($9.99 CAD). MorForm Manager is $34.99 USD ($49.99 CAD). MorForm Office is $299.99 CAD and includes a 15-day free trial. Pay once and own what you buy, with no hidden fees.
Does it work offline on job sites?
Yes. All three apps work fully offline. Log time, fill FLHA and safety forms, view job details, and build estimates with no internet connection. Data syncs by email or your connected cloud folder when you're back in range.
How do the apps communicate with each other?
Two ways, your choice. By email, managers dispatch jobs as .morjob files, supervisors report back as .morrep files, and Manager and MorForm Office sync full snapshots via .mordata files. Or connect a shared Company Cloud Folder in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive. MorForm runs no servers of its own and never sees your cloud login.
Does MorForm store my data in the cloud?
Only if you choose to. By default your data stays on your device and moves as email attachments. Optionally, connect a shared folder in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive so the crew's devices sync automatically. That folder is yours. MorForm has no servers and never sees your cloud account.
