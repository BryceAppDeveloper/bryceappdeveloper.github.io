How it works

No server in the middle

The apps exchange jobs, reports and data as small files — by email, or through a shared folder you connect in your company's own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive. MorForm runs no servers of its own; either way the files stay in your accounts and on your devices.

Why not a spreadsheet?

Old way vs. MorForm

Construction sites have run on paper, email threads, and spreadsheets for decades. MorForm puts it all in one loop.

The old way
  • Hours tracked on paper, re-entered in Excel at the end of the week
  • Safety forms printed, signed by hand, scanned and emailed as JPGs
  • Job estimates live in a spreadsheet only one person can update
  • Report emails with 10 loose attachments — no standard format
  • Another SaaS tool billed monthly, whether the job is running or not
MorForm
  • Time logged on site in seconds, exported as a signed PDF in one tap
  • FLHA / JHA filled digitally — safety PDFs route to the right inbox automatically
  • Estimate builds the dispatch package — no re-entry, no version drift
  • One .morrep file: time, expenses, safety forms — all in one attachment
  • One-time purchase — no renewal, no MorForm account, no monthly bill ever

Common Questions

Does MorForm require a subscription? +

No. MorForm is a one-time purchase. MorForm (Supervisor) is $6.99 USD ($9.99 CAD). MorForm Manager is $10.99 USD ($14.99 CAD). MorForm Office is $189.99 CAD (USD pricing coming soon). Pay once, own it forever — no subscription, no renewal, no hidden fees.

Does it work offline on job sites? +

Yes. All three apps work fully offline. Log time, fill FLHA and safety forms, view job details, and build estimates with no internet connection. Data syncs by email or your connected cloud folder when you're back in range.

How do the apps communicate with each other? +

Two ways — your choice. By email, managers dispatch jobs as .morjob files, supervisors report back as .morrep files, and Manager and MorForm Office sync full snapshots via .mordata files. Or connect a shared Company Cloud Folder in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive and the apps read and write the same files there automatically. MorForm runs no servers of its own and never sees your cloud login.

Does MorForm store my data in the cloud? +

Only if you choose to. By default your data stays on your device and moves as email attachments. Optionally, connect a shared folder in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive so the crew's devices sync automatically. That folder is yours — MorForm has no servers and never sees your cloud account; your data is governed by your cloud provider's terms.

What safety forms are included? +

MorForm includes FLHA (Field Level Hazard Assessment), JHA (Job Hazard Analysis), Emergency Response Plan, Site Inspection, and Incident Report forms. Both Alberta OH&S and US OSHA jurisdiction references are built in.

What platforms does MorForm support? +

MorForm (supervisor) and MorForm Manager run on iOS and Android. MorForm Office runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11. All three apps communicate through email or a shared cloud folder you connect — no MorForm account required.

Does MorForm work in the United States? +

Yes. The Supervisor app includes both Alberta OH&S and US OSHA jurisdiction modes — field-level terminology (FLHA vs. JHA, OH&S vs. OSHA) adjusts to whichever you choose during setup. Time tracking, expenses, and job management work the same everywhere.

What happens if I lose my phone or switch devices? +

Use the built-in backup: Settings → Data Backup → Create Backup saves a file you can email to yourself or save to cloud storage. Restoring on a new device takes a few taps. Since there's no server, the backup file is your data — keep a copy somewhere safe at the end of each pay period.
