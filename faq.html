FAQ

Straight answers

Pricing, offline use, syncing, safety forms, and where your data lives, answered plainly. Anything else: support@morform.app.

How much does MorForm cost?
MorForm is a one-time purchase. MorForm (Supervisor) is $6.99 USD ($9.99 CAD). MorForm Manager is $34.99 USD ($49.99 CAD). MorForm Office is $299.99 CAD and includes a 15-day free trial. Pay once and own what you buy, with no hidden fees.
Does it work offline on job sites?
Yes. All three apps work fully offline. Log time, fill FLHA and safety forms, view job details, and build estimates with no internet connection. Data syncs by email or your connected cloud folder when you're back in range.
How do the apps communicate with each other?
Two ways, your choice. By email, managers dispatch jobs as .morjob files, supervisors report back as .morrep files, and Manager and MorForm Office sync full snapshots via .mordata files. Or connect a shared Company Cloud Folder in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive. MorForm runs no servers of its own and never sees your cloud login. See how it works, file by file →
Does MorForm store my data in the cloud?
Only if you choose to. By default your data stays on your device and moves as email attachments. Optionally, connect a shared folder in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive so the crew's devices sync automatically. That folder is yours. MorForm has no servers and never sees your cloud account.
Which safety forms are included?
The Supervisor app includes FLHA / JHA (field level hazard assessment), Emergency Response Plan, Site Hazard Inspection with automatic compliance scoring, and Incident Report, each generating a signed, branded PDF. Blank printable versions are also free on the site, no app required: free FLHA form.
Which OHS jurisdictions are supported?
Canadian provinces and territories (including the Federal Canada Labour Code) and US OSHA. Your jurisdiction sets the OHS legislation referenced on safety forms and the overtime rules used by timesheets, for example Alberta OHS with daily >8h and weekly >44h overtime.
Do my subtrades need the app to sign safety forms?
No. Supervisors can send interactive safety forms to subtrades who don't have MorForm; they fill and sign in the browser, no app or account needed on their end.
Can I get my data out?
Yes, at any time. Reports export as PDF and CSV, estimates exchange with Excel (XLSX), Office exports to QuickBooks, and every app can save a full backup file you keep wherever you like. Your data never depends on MorForm existing.
