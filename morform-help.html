Welcome to MorForm

What MorForm does MorForm is your construction field companion. It tracks your time, expenses, and safety forms on site — and communicates with your Project Manager's app through regular email or a shared cloud folder you connect. No MorForm account, no servers, no subscriptions.
TabWhat it does
🕐 TimeLog daily hours by job site
🔨 JobsManage job sites, view plans and contacts
💲 ExpensesTrack job costs and receipts
🛡 SafetyComplete OH&S forms — FLHA, ERP, Inspection, Incident
📊 ReportReview and send your pay period report to your PM
ℹ️MorForm works with MorForm Manager — your Project Manager's app. Your PM dispatches jobs to you and you send reports back, by email or through a shared Company Cloud Folder in your own Drive/Dropbox/OneDrive. MorForm runs no server of its own.

First-Time Setup

When you first open MorForm the Setup Wizard runs automatically. It takes about 3 minutes. Every setting here can be changed later in Settings.

1
Welcome
Tap Get Started.
2
Your Company
Enter your company name, phone, and address. Tap the logo area to upload your company logo — it appears on every PDF and safety form you generate.
3
Your Info
Your full name (required), work email, and phone number. Your name appears on all safety forms, timesheets, and reports.
4
Pay Schedule
Choose Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Semi-Monthly, or Monthly. This controls how the app groups entries into pay periods for reporting.
5
Work Hours & Reminders
Set your typical start and end times, which days you work, and your time entry increment (15 min, 30 min, or 1 hour). Toggle daily hour reminders, morning FLHA prompts, and pay period notifications.
6
Jurisdiction
Choose your country and province/state. This sets the OHS authority referenced on your safety forms, configures overtime thresholds, and enables stat holiday auto-detection. Canadian users see FLHA and OH&S references; US users see JHA/JSA and OSHA references.
7
Report Recipients
Enter emails for: Manager/Supervisor (timesheets), Project Manager (your PM's MorForm Manager app), Accounting (expense reports), Safety Manager (safety form PDFs), and your own Work Email (receives completed subtrade forms).
8
Done
Your profile is saved. Add job sites in the Jobs tab, or wait for your PM to dispatch a job to you.
💡Re-run the wizard anytime: Settings → Data & Backup → Re-run Setup Wizard. Your existing data is not affected.

Logging Time

Adding a Time Entry

1
Tap + Add Entry
The entry form slides up. Make sure the correct day is selected first.
2
Select the job site
Choose from your active jobs list. Tap + Quick Job to create one on the spot if it isn't there yet.
3
Set the hours
Tap the hours field to open the picker. Increment is set in Settings → Work & Pay (15 min / 30 min / 1 hour).
4
Mark Overtime if applicable
Toggle Mark as Overtime. The app warns you if adding these hours would push the daily threshold.
5
Mark Stat Holiday if applicable
Toggle Stat Holiday. The entry shows a gold ★ badge. Hours worked on a stat are automatically considered overtime.
6
Tap Save Entry
The entry is saved. Daily and weekly totals update immediately.
⚠️Always select the correct job site. Hours are tracked per job and flow into your PM's budget vs. actual reports when you submit.

Editing and Deleting Entries

Tap the ✏️ pencil on any entry to edit it. Tap 🗑️ to delete — you'll be asked to confirm. You can change the job, hours, OT flag, or stat flag at any time before sending your report.

Day Notes

Tap + Note on any selected day to add a reminder visible only to you — site access code changes, equipment delivery reminders, shift notes.

OT Thresholds

Daily and weekly OT thresholds are set in Settings → Work & Pay. Alberta default is 8h/day. The app warns you when a new entry would push a job over the daily threshold — but you always decide.

Job Detail Tabs

Tap any job card to open the job detail. Four tabs across the top:

TabWhat's there
📋 InfoAddress, dates, PM contact, permits, plans, photos and notes
✅ ScopeThe job's scope of work as a checklist — grouped by division and trade
👥 ContactsTrade and site contacts — tap 📞 to call or ✉️ to email

Scope Checklist

When your PM dispatches a job with a scope of work, it arrives as a checklist in the ✅ Scope tab — divisions collapse into trades, trades into individual items. Check items off as the work gets done. Each job card shows a scope progress bar so you can see how far along a job is at a glance, and your checked-off progress is included automatically the next time you send that job's .morrep report — your PM sees exactly what's complete.

Pinning Jobs

Long-press any job card and tap 📌 Pin to keep it at the top of your Jobs list. Long-press again to unpin.

FLHA / JHA — Step by Step

The Field Level Hazard Assessment (FLHA) / Job Hazard Analysis (JHA) is required every working day. It's a 5-page guided form.

Need a paper or browser version for someone without the app? Get the free printable FLHA form on our website — fill it online or download the PDF.

1
Page 1 — Job Info
Select the job site. Enter the date (or date range for consecutive days). List all trades and workers on site today. Describe today's full scope of work.
2
Page 2 — Hazards
Tap each hazard present on site today: heavy loads, electrical, working at height, slippery surfaces, pinch points, and more. For each hazard, enter the control measure you're implementing.
3
Page 3 — Task Steps
Break today's work into individual steps. For each step, describe the associated hazards and how you're eliminating or controlling them.
4
Page 4 — PPE Required
Tap each piece of PPE required on site today. Hard hat, safety glasses, high-vis vest, steel-toed boots, fall protection, gloves, respirator, and more.
5
Page 5 — Signatures
Type your name to sign. Tap + Add Another Signature for each crew member, subtrade, or visitor on site. Everyone present must sign.
6
Tap Save FLHA
The form appears in your Safety list. Tap it to view the PDF, email it to your Safety Manager, or extend it.
💡Extending a FLHA: Open the saved FLHA and tap Extend — Add New Day & Signatures. This adds a new date and signature block to the existing form rather than creating a duplicate. The full original assessment — every hazard, control, task step, PPE item and prior signature — is shown on screen before the signature fields, so each person can read the complete document before signing.
ℹ️The PDF includes your company logo, all hazards and controls listed, task steps, full PPE checklist, and all signatures with dates. It's a legally defensible document — be thorough.

Emergency Response Plan

The ERP is required once per job before work begins. It must be posted on site and communicated to all workers (OHS Code Part 7).

1
Tap ERP on the Safety screen
2
Enter job and site details
Job site, site address, and the date.
3
Emergency contacts
Names and phone numbers for the Site Supervisor, Owner/PM, and any other emergency contacts.
4
Nearest hospital
Name, address, and phone number of the nearest hospital or emergency clinic.
5
Muster point
Describe the assembly point where all workers gather in an emergency.
6
Emergency procedures
Describe the procedure for each emergency type: fire, injury, environmental spill, power outage, and severe weather.
7
Tap Save ERP
The form is saved and the ERP badge on the job card clears. Email the PDF to your Safety Manager and post a printed copy on site.
⚠️The ERP must be communicated to every worker on site before work begins. Update it if the site address, hospital route, or emergency contacts change.

Site Hazard Inspection

A formal 10-category site inspection is required before work begins on a new job and at least weekly thereafter (OHS Code Part 2 & 36).

The 10 categories: Housekeeping & Site Organization, Signage & Barricading, PPE, Tools & Equipment, Electrical Safety, Fall Protection, Ladders & Scaffolding, Fire Safety, Hazardous Materials, and First Aid & Emergency.

1
Page 1 — Site Info
Select the job, enter the date, and add the inspector name.
2
Pages 2–11 — Category by category
For each item tap Pass ✓, Fail ✗, or N/A. Tap the note icon on any failed item to add a corrective action note.
3
Final page — Signatures & Summary
Review the compliance score. Failed items with required corrective actions are listed. Sign and add any additional notes.
4
Tap Save Inspection
Saved to your Safety list. Email the PDF to your Safety Manager.
ℹ️The compliance score excludes N/A items so it accurately reflects applicable items. A score below 80% typically requires immediate corrective action before work continues.

Incident Report

Complete an Incident Report for any injury, near miss, property damage, or dangerous occurrence. File immediately — do not delay.

1
Page 1 — Incident Info
Date, time, location, and a description of what happened. Select the incident type: injury, near miss, property damage, or environmental.
2
Page 2 — People Involved
Name and role of each person involved or injured. Body part affected. Type of medical treatment required.
3
Page 3 — Cause & Contributing Factors
Describe the root cause and any contributing factors. Critical for prevention.
4
Page 4 — Corrective Actions
What immediate action was taken? What corrective action will prevent recurrence? Who is responsible and by when?
5
Page 5 — Witnesses & Signatures
List any witnesses. Sign and date. Add signatures from anyone involved.
6
Tap Save
The report is saved to your Safety list. A persistent alert appears on the Safety tab until it has been emailed.
7
Open the saved report → Email
Tap 📧 Email to Safety Manager & PM. Your mail app opens pre-addressed to both your Safety Manager and your Project Manager with the PDF attached. Incident reports are the only safety form that goes to both.
🚨Do not delay filing an incident report. The app shows a persistent alert until it has been emailed. Your obligation under the applicable OHS authority exists regardless of how minor the incident appears.
ℹ️Your Project Manager will receive the incident PDF and is required to review it and record corrective actions in their MorForm Manager or MorForm Office app. This creates the written investigation record required by Alberta OH&S.

How Sending Works

MorForm has no backend server and requires no MorForm account. Data moves as file attachments — by email, or through an optional shared Company Cloud Folder you connect in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive. Nothing syncs to any cloud unless you set that up; email always works on its own.

To use a cloud folder: have the folder synced on your phone (your cloud app does this), then in MorForm tap Connect Company Cloud Folder and pick it — dispatched jobs land automatically and your reports drop straight into the right inbox. On iPhone, use Settings → Import from Cloud instead. Your office sets the folder up once (see the Office guide).

Working With Your PM

MorForm and MorForm Manager are two separate apps that communicate through email. Here's the complete loop:

1
PM builds the job
Creates the job in MorForm Manager with plans, trade contacts, permits, and scope notes.
2
PM dispatches to you
Taps Dispatch → your email arrives with a .morjob attachment.
3
You import the job
Tap the .morjob file → MorForm opens and imports everything including plans, trade contacts, permit numbers, and photos.
4
You work the job
Log time, expenses, and complete safety forms — all linked to the dispatched job.
5
Send a job report
Open the job in the Jobs tab → tap 📤 Send → confirm → .morrep email sent to your PM → PM taps the attachment → imports to their Inbox.
6
PM approves
Your hours and expenses commit to the job's budget vs. actual tracking. Job statuses update automatically in your PM's app.
ℹ️Your PM can see a summary of how many safety forms you completed each period (counts by type) but not the actual form content — except for Incident Reports, which are emailed directly to your PM as well as your Safety Manager.

Tips & Troubleshooting

The .morjob file didn't open MorForm

Tap the 📥 Import button on the Jobs screen, or go to Settings → Data & Backup → Import Job File. If it still doesn't auto-open, uninstall and reinstall once — Android registers file associations on install.

☁️ Company Cloud Folder (optional)

If your company shares a Drive / Dropbox / OneDrive folder, you can send and receive through it instead of email. Install your cloud app so the folder syncs on this phone, then Settings → Data & Backup → Import from Cloud pulls in jobs (or just tap a file in your cloud app). Tap "How do I set this up?" there for a step-by-step walkthrough. Email keeps working alongside it.

Moving to a new phone

On your old phone: Settings → Data & Backup → Email Backup emails you the .morbak file as an attachment, or Save Backup to Device saves it to Files / Drive. On your new phone: install MorForm, then on the first setup screen tap "Import from Backup" (or tap the .morbak attachment in your email) to restore everything — jobs, time, expenses, safety docs and settings. A .morbak backup is your whole app; a .morjob file is just a single job from your PM. If MorForm ever can't read your saved data on launch, it now shows a recovery screen with Try Again and Restore from Backup instead of starting empty, so a one-time read glitch can't wipe your data.

I logged hours on the wrong job

Tap ✏️ on the entry in the Time tab, change the job site, and tap Update. Do this before sending your report.

I deleted a job but need it back

Ask your PM to re-send the job's .morjob file. Importing it restores the job from Recently Deleted — your time entries, expenses, and safety forms for that job come back with it, updated with the latest dispatch details.

Report says "nothing to send"

Check your pay period date range at the top of the Report tab. Tap the dates to adjust them manually.

FLHA reminder keeps appearing every morning

Complete a FLHA for today in the Safety tab. To turn off the reminder permanently: Settings → Notifications → Morning FLHA Reminder.

Subtrade says they didn't receive the form

Most email providers block .html file attachments. Resend using the share sheet and choose WhatsApp, AirDrop, or save to Files and share from there.

Best Practices

  • 📅 Log hours at the end of every working day
  • 🛡 Complete your FLHA before work begins each morning
  • 🚒 Complete the ERP before starting on any new job site
  • 💾 Back up your data at the end of each pay period
  • 📧 Send your report as soon as the pay period ends
  • 📷 Add a site photo when something notable happens on site
  • 🔄 Keep the app updated from the Play Store or App Store
