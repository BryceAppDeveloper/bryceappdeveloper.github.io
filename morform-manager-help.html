Welcome to MorForm Manager

Your field command centre. MorForm Manager runs the field side of your projects: spec jobs on site, dispatch work to supervisors, track budget vs. actuals, and manage your team. Estimating and quoting happen in MorForm Office; Manager sends job requests there. No MorForm account, and no servers. Communication happens by email or a shared cloud folder you connect.

FeatureWhat it does
Jobs DashboardFull pipeline: Quoting → Approved → In Progress → Complete
Job SpecsScope a job on site (type → subtype → trades & scope from your saved library) and send it to the office to estimate
DispatchSend job info, plans, contacts, permits, and photos to supervisors by email, or dropped straight into their cloud inbox folder
InboxEverything coming in: supervisor reports (.morrep) and office updates (.mordata, including client approvals). Approving a report commits actuals to budgets
OutboxEverything going out: specs to the office, job dispatches to supervisors, full data handoffs
Crew DirectorySupervisor roster with full history and safety compliance tracking
ReportsAggregate hours and expenses by job or supervisor across any date range

First-Time Setup

The Setup Wizard runs automatically on first launch. Takes about 3 minutes.

  1. Welcome

    Tap Get Started.

  2. Your Company

    Company name, phone, address, and logo. Your logo prints on every PDF the app generates. Use a clean, high-resolution image.

  3. Your Profile

    Your name, email, and phone. Supervisors see this as the PM contact on every job you dispatch to them. They tap your name to call or email you directly from the job screen.

  4. App Colour

    Choose an accent colour for the interface: Manager Gold, Navy, Steel Blue, Teal, Forest, Brick, and more. Purely visual.

  5. Report Routing

    Optional: enter email addresses for Accounting (forwarded expense summaries) and Safety Manager (forwarded safety summaries).

  6. Done

    Add supervisors in the Crew tab first, then create your first job.

💡Add supervisors in the Crew tab before creating jobs so you can assign and dispatch to them immediately.

Jobs Dashboard

Creating a Job

Tap + Create in the top-right to open the job form: name, address, start and end dates, scope notes, supervisor assignment, contacts, permits, site photos, and documents. After saving a new job you're offered Build Spec. One tap drops you into the spec builder with that job attached. To create multiple jobs at once, tap 📊 Import beside the + Create button and pick a CSV or XLSX file using the MorForm Job Template (available in Settings → Data & Backup).

Approval & Dispatch Badges

Job cards show pills beside the status chip: ⏳ AWAITING APPROVAL (spec sent to the office, no client approval back yet), ✅ APPROVED (the office's .mordata confirmed the estimate was won), and 📤 DISPATCHED (job package sent to a supervisor).

Job Status Pipeline

Tap the status chip on any job card to change it manually.

Quoting → Approved → In Progress → On Hold → Complete

People & Trades

Site Contacts

Add contacts via Edit Job → Contacts → + Add, or directly from the Job → People tab → + Add Contact. Tapping Add Contact expands an inline form in the People tab: fill in name, role, email, and phone, then tap Add Contact to save.

Trade Contacts from Job Specs

Each trade in a job spec carries a vendor/company plus a contact (name, phone, email) that flows to the supervisor's Trades on Site list, and its scope items that flow to the supervisor so they know what each trade is on site to do. Tap any trade card in the People tab to expand it, fill in the Vendor / Company and contact fields (this is where a trade gets its company attached once quotes start coming in), then tap Save Contact. Tap 📞/✉️ to call or email directly from the card. Notes added in the spec builder (📝 on a trade or scope item) show on the card read-only.

ℹ️All trade contacts, even ones with no contact info yet, are included in the dispatch package. Supervisors see the full trade list on site including scope of work. When a supervisor marks a trade's scope complete, that status comes back in their .morrep report.
📤

Dispatching Jobs

Once a job is ready to go to site, tap 📤 Dispatch on the job detail. A dispatch picker lets you choose what to include and who to send it to.

What's in the Dispatch Package

Job name & addressStart & end datesScope notes & lock box codesYour PM contactAll site contactsTrade contacts & scope of workPermits & datesSite photosPlans & documents (PDF)

  1. Tap 📤 Dispatch on the job detail

  2. Select a supervisor

    Choose from your Crew directory. Their email pre-fills in the email composer.

  3. Choose what to include

    Everything is selected by default. Deselect individual contacts, photos, or documents if needed.

  4. Tap Send Job Package

    Your mail app opens pre-addressed with the .morjob file attached.

  5. Send the email

    Supervisor taps the attachment → MorForm opens and imports everything. They see a confirmation listing what was included.

⚠️If the package exceeds 15MB you'll be warned. Large PDFs are the usual cause. Share large plans via a file service and reference them in the scope notes instead.

Job Specs

The Job Specs tab is your on-site scoping tool. You define what a job is (type, subtype, trades, and scope) and send it to MorForm Office, where it becomes an estimate (and the job, once it's won). Pricing and quoting happen in Office; Manager stays focused on the field.

Building a Spec (job first)

  1. Start with the job

    Enter the new job: only a name is required; address, client name, phone, email, and notes can wait. Or pick an existing job (e.g. a site visit for added scope). Its current spec pre-fills the next steps.

  2. Pick the job type

    Residential, Commercial, or Industrial.

  3. Pick or create a subtype

    e.g. "House build", "Dental clinic", "Whitebox". Subtypes you create are saved under that job type forever.

  4. Toggle trades & scope, then save

    The subtype's saved trades appear with their scope items. Toggle off what this job doesn't need, add what it does. Every trade you've ever used (across ALL job types and subtypes) is one tap away as a ＋ chip; new scope items are saved back to the subtype.

💡The app starts as a blank slate and gets faster the more you use it. The first dental clinic you spec is all typing; the fifth is all tapping, because your trades and scope items are already in the library.

Editing a Spec

Tap any spec card to reopen it right at Trades & scope: add a trade you forgot, extend scope, keep working on a half-finished spec. Everything survives the round trip: trade contacts, the supervisor's scope progress, and your notes. Going back to re-pick a subtype warns before replacing what you've built.

⚠️Editing a spec that was already sent marks it unsent. The Outbox badge prompts you to re-send the updated version to the office.

Notes & Vendors

Tap 📝 on any trade or scope item to attach a job-specific note ("use existing panel", "client supplies fixtures"). The icon lights up when a note is there, and the note travels with the spec to the office and with the dispatch to the supervisor, but it's never saved into your library, so it won't haunt the next job. Vendor and contact details are tucked behind + vendor / contact on each trade. Skip them while scoping; attach the company later from the job's People tab when quotes come in.

Sending to the Office

Tap 📤 Send to Office on any spec card (or from the Outbox in the Transfer tab). A small .mordata file goes by email; the office opens it in MorForm Office (File → Import Data from Manager) and the spec lands as a job request. One tap there converts it into a full estimate: every trade becomes a section with its scope checklist attached.

ℹ️When the client approves and the estimate is marked Won in Office, the office sends a .mordata back. Import it (Inbox → Import from Office) and the job turns ✅ APPROVED. Until then it shows ⏳ AWAITING APPROVAL.
ℹ️The spec's trades and scope items are also what dispatch sends to the supervisor. When they tick off scope on site, their .morrep report updates the spec's progress here.
ℹ️Estimates, PDF quotes, client invoicing, and change orders now live in MorForm Office. See the Office guide.

Tap + Add to add a supervisor manually. Supervisors are also added automatically the first time they send you a .morrep report. Their name, email, and phone come from their MorForm profile.

📥

Inbox: Receiving

The Inbox receives from both directions: supervisor reports (.morrep) and office data (.mordata). When a supervisor sends a job report, tap the .morrep attachment in your email. MorForm Manager opens and imports it automatically. Or tap Import in the Inbox (Transfer tab) to pick the file manually.

Import from Office

Tap 🏢 Import from Office (.mordata) to bring in the office's data, most importantly client approvals. When an estimate is marked Won in MorForm Office, its .mordata marks the matching job here ✅ APPROVED.

⚠️A job whose spec was sent to the office but isn't approved yet warns before accepting a .morrep ("Import Anyway" is available). Jobs that never went through the office aren't affected.
ℹ️Reports are sent per job, not per pay period. When a supervisor taps 📤 Send on a job card, it sends all time, expenses, and safety docs for that specific job. You may receive multiple reports per supervisor if they work across several jobs.
💡If a supervisor re-sends a report you already imported, you're prompted to Replace it with the newer version. If the earlier version was already approved, its committed hours and expenses are backed out automatically. Approve the replacement to commit the updated totals. No double counting.

What's in a Report

DataDetails
Time entriesEvery hour by date and job site, with OT and stat holiday flags
ExpensesAll amounts by category with reimbursable total
Safety summaryCount of each form type, not the actual PDFs
Incident reportsFull incident details included when present; triggers the sign-off workflow
Job statusesCurrent status of dispatched jobs; your pipeline updates on approval
Trade scope completionsWhich trades have had their scope marked complete by the supervisor
Unmatched jobsHours on jobs not dispatched from your app, flagged but still visible

Incident Report Sign-off

🚨When a report contains an incident report, a red 🚨 INCIDENT badge appears on the report card. Alberta OH&S requires the employer to investigate and document corrective actions. The sign-off in MorForm creates this record.

Open the report, and a dedicated red sign-off section appears below the safety documents. For each incident:

  1. Review the incident description

    The supervisor's incident details, date, location, and notes are shown.

  2. Enter corrective actions

    Describe what was done, root cause, who is responsible for follow-up, and by when.

  3. Tap ✅ Sign Off

    Records your name, timestamp, and notes permanently. This is your investigation record. It can be provided to an OHS officer on request.

ℹ️Incident sign-off is separate from report approval. Sign off the incident first, then approve the full report. Sign-off cannot be undone.

Approving a Report

  1. Review the report

    Check hours by job, expense amounts, and any unmatched job warnings.

  2. Sign off any incidents first

    If a red 🚨 section appears, complete the incident sign-off before approving.

  3. Tap ✓ Approve & Commit

    Hours and expenses commit to job actuals. Job statuses update. Supervisor is auto-added to Crew directory if new. Report is marked reviewed.

🚨Approval cannot be undone. Hours and expenses lock into the job cost record permanently. Review carefully before approving, especially for unmatched job IDs and unusually large expenses.

Reports & Actuals

Tap 📊 Reports in the header. Set a date range. It defaults to the current month. Three views:

ViewShows
SummaryTotal hours, OT hours, total expenses, reimbursable total, safety doc count, number of supervisors and submissions for the date range
By JobHours and expenses rolled up per job site. OT shown separately. For billing and profitability.
By SupervisorHours, OT, expenses, reimbursable amounts, and report count per supervisor. For payroll review.

Tap the CSV button on any view to download a spreadsheet of the filtered data.

💡The budget progress bar on each job detail updates automatically each time you approve a supervisor report. Check it regularly: it turns orange at 80% and red at 100% of the estimated budget.

Time: Hours & Daytimer

The Time tab has two halves, switched with the toggle at the top: Log Hours and the Daytimer calendar.

Log Hours

A weekly timesheet: the same per-job, per-day logger the supervisor app uses. Pick a day on the strip, tap + Add Entry, choose a job site and hours, and flag Overtime or Stat Holiday. Each day also takes a quick note or can be marked a day off.

Daytimer: Month / Week / Day

A scheduling calendar with three views. Tap any day to open it and add a day note, timed Events (a start and end time, colour-coded by category) and quick Reminders (a single time). The Day view is a 15-minute time grid: tap an empty slot to drop a new event right there, granular to the quarter-hour.

Week board: your week at a glance

The Week view is a board with a row per day. Scroll the whole week and read each day from its colour-coded blocks: logged hours (green, purple if overtime), events (by category), reminders (amber) and the day note (slate). Assigned crew shows on the event blocks. Tap any block to open and edit; tap ＋ on a day to add.

Crew dispatch

On any event you can pick a job and assign a crew member. Their name shows on the calendar block, so the Daytimer doubles as a dispatch board for who's where, and when.

Reminders (push + alarm)

Turn on the 🔔 Remind me toggle on any event or reminder and pick a lead time: at time, or 5 / 10 / 15 / 30 minutes or 1 hour before. It fires both a push notification and a loud ⏰ alarm-style notification; unfinished due reminders show a badge on the Time tab and the app icon.

ℹ️The alarm is a notification with an alarm tone: on Android it plays on your alarm volume; on iPhone it breaks through Focus but cannot override the mute switch (only the phone's own Clock app can do that).
ℹ️Reminders need notification permission. The app asks the first time you save one. If you said no, enable notifications for MorForm Manager in your phone's settings.
💡Moved here from the old Tasks tab: your tasks are now Daytimer reminders, and job-specific notes live on each job. Open a job and tap its Notes tab.

How Both Apps Connect

MorForm Manager and MorForm (the supervisor app) are completely independent. No shared server, and no MorForm account. Data moves as files using three custom file types: by email, or through an optional shared Company Cloud Folder in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive. On Android, tap Connect Company Cloud Folder in Manager to send and receive automatically; on iPhone use Import from Cloud. Email always works on its own.

FileDirectionContains
.morjobYou → SupervisorJob, dates, scope, PM contact, all contacts, trade contacts, permits, photos, documents
.morrepSupervisor → You + OfficeTime entries, expenses, safety doc summaries, job statuses, trade scope completions; the same file imports into both apps against the same job
.mordataManager ↔ OfficeData snapshot: jobs, job specs / job requests, budgets, PMs, crew, notes. Specs go to Office for estimating; Office sends back client approvals (estimate Won → job ✅ APPROVED in Manager).

Other emails supervisors send

WhatGoes to
Safety form PDFs (FLHA, ERP, Inspection)Their Safety Manager
Incident Report PDFsTheir Safety Manager and you (Project Manager); triggers sign-off workflow in your app
Timesheet PDF + CSVTheir Manager/Supervisor email
Expense report PDF + CSVTheir Accounting email
Progress photos + notesYou (Project Manager)
Interactive blank formsSubtrades (who submit back to supervisor + safety manager)
ℹ️Your supervisor's .morrep report includes safety doc counts (FLHA: 10, Inspection: 2, etc.) but not the actual PDF forms. Safety PDFs go directly to the Safety Manager. You see compliance numbers, not form content.

Send to Office / Handoff

MorForm Manager can export your full data snapshot and send it to MorForm Office on your desktop, or hand off a project to another project manager.

  1. Open Settings → Data & Backup

  2. Tap 🖥 Send to Office / Handoff

    Your own email is selected by default. Tap "Choose PMs" to also send to project managers from your PM list.

  3. Mail app opens pre-addressed

    The summary and the .mordata file are already attached. Just hit send.

  4. Recipient imports the file

    On MorForm Office: Settings → Data & Backup → 📱 Import .mordata. On another Manager mobile: tap the attachment directly.

💡The .mordata file contains all jobs, specs, budgets, PMs, crew, and notes. Choose Merge to add new items alongside existing data, or Replace All to fully swap, with a double confirmation before proceeding.

Import from Office or Another Manager

Tap 📦 Import from Manager / Office in Settings → Data & Backup, or tap a .mordata email attachment directly. You'll be offered Merge or Replace All.

⚠️Factory Reset permanently deletes all data: all jobs, specs, crew history, and submissions. Back up your data first using Email Backup or Save Backup to Device in Data & Backup.

☁️ Company Cloud Folder (optional)

If your company shares a Drive / Dropbox / OneDrive folder, you can send and receive through it instead of email. Install your cloud app so the folder syncs on this phone, then tap Settings → Data & Backup → Connect Company Cloud Folder. Supervisor reports and office data then import automatically each time you open the app, and jobs you dispatch are dropped into each supervisor's inbox folder. Import from Cloud still pulls files in manually, or just tap a file in your cloud app. Tap "How do I set this up?" there for a walkthrough. Email keeps working alongside it.

Sending is nudged along too: right after a dispatch, spec or handoff lands in the folder, MorForm briefly opens your cloud app (on iPhone, the Files app at your company folder) so the new file uploads right away instead of waiting for the phone to get around to it. Swipe back once it opens. You can turn this off in Settings → Data & Backup with the "Auto-open cloud app after send" toggle.

Moving to a New Phone

On your old phone tap Email Backup to email yourself the .morbak file (it arrives as an attachment), or Save Backup to Device to save it to Files / Drive. On the new phone, install MorForm Manager and on the first setup screen tap Import from Backup (or tap the .morbak attachment in your email) to restore everything, including job photos, documents, imported report photos and safety pages (the actual files ride inside the backup). A .morbak backup is your whole app and is different from a .mordata data-handoff file.

ℹ️If the app ever can't read your saved data on launch, it now shows a recovery screen with Try Again and Restore from Backup instead of starting empty. A one-time read glitch can't wipe your data.
