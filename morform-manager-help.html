Welcome to MorForm Manager
Your project command centre MorForm Manager handles the full project lifecycle — estimate jobs, dispatch work to supervisors, track budget vs. actuals, and manage your team. No accounts, no cloud, no subscriptions. All communication happens through email.
FeatureWhat it does
Jobs DashboardFull pipeline: Quoting → Approved → In Progress → Complete
Estimates & QuotesCSI-division quote builder — labour, externals, markup, allowances, extras
Client InvoicingGenerate internal summary PDF or clean client invoice, email direct to client
DispatchSend job info, plans, contacts, permits, and photos to supervisors in one email
InboxReceive and approve supervisor pay period reports — commits actuals to budgets
Crew DirectorySupervisor roster with full history and safety compliance tracking
ReportsAggregate hours and expenses by job or supervisor across any date range

First-Time Setup

The Setup Wizard runs automatically on first launch. Takes about 3 minutes.

1
Welcome
Tap Get Started.
2
Your Company
Company name, phone, address, and logo. Your logo prints on every quote PDF and client invoice — use a clean, high-resolution image.
3
Your Profile
Your name, email, and phone. Supervisors see this as the PM contact on every job you dispatch to them. They tap your name to call or email you directly from the job screen.
4
App Colour

5

💡Add supervisors in the Crew tab before creating jobs so you can assign and dispatch to them immediately.

Jobs Dashboard

Creating a Job

Tap + Create in the top-right to open the job form: name, address, start and end dates, scope notes, supervisor assignment, contacts, permits, site photos, and documents. To create multiple jobs at once, tap 📊 Import beside the + Create button and pick a CSV or XLSX file using the MorForm Job Template (available in Settings → Data & Backup).

Job Status Pipeline

Tap the status chip on any job card to change it manually. Status also updates automatically when you change the estimate status.

Quoting → Approved → In Progress → On Hold → Complete

Permits

Add and manage permits in the Edit Job form (tap ✏️ Edit → scroll to Permits → + Add). Each permit has:

FieldDescription
TypeBuilding, Demolition, Electrical, Plumbing, Mechanical, Fire Suppression, Development, Occupancy, or Other
StatusPending → Applied → Issued → Closed — tap to change
Permit #The number from the issuing authority (e.g. BLD-2025-0042)
Issuing AuthorityCity, county, or municipality
Applied / Issued / ExpiresKey lifecycle dates
Inspection DateOptional — when the inspector is scheduled on site
NotesInspector name, permit conditions, follow-up actions
ℹ️Permits with Pending or Applied status show an orange ⚠ Permits Pending banner on the job Overview tab. Permits are included in the dispatch package — supervisors see permit numbers and inspection dates on site.

People & Trades

Site Contacts

Add contacts via Edit Job → Contacts → + Add, or directly from the Job → People tab → + Add Contact. Tapping Add Contact expands an inline form in the People tab — fill in name, role, email, and phone, then tap Add Contact to save.

Trade Contacts from Estimates

When you add a subtrade in Estimates → Externals, each row has a Trade Contact field. Their name, phone, and email appear in the People tab under Trades on Site. Each row also has a Scope of Work field — this flows through to the supervisor's job screen so they know exactly what each trade is on site to do.

ℹ️All trade contacts are included in the dispatch package. When a supervisor marks a trade's scope complete, that status comes back in their .morrep report.
📤
Dispatching Jobs

Once a job is ready to go to site, tap 📤 Dispatch on the job detail. A dispatch picker lets you choose what to include and who to send it to.

What's in the Dispatch Package

Job name & address Start & end dates Scope notes & lock box codes Your PM contact All site contacts Trade contacts & scope of work Permits & dates Site photos Plans & documents (PDF)
1
Tap 📤 Dispatch on the job detail
2
Select a supervisor
Choose from your Crew directory. Their email pre-fills in the email composer.
3
Choose what to include
Everything is selected by default. Deselect individual contacts, photos, or documents if needed.
4
Tap Send Job Package
Your mail app opens pre-addressed with the .morjob file attached.
5
Send the email
Supervisor taps the attachment → MorForm opens and imports everything. They see a confirmation listing what was included.
⚠️If the package exceeds 15MB you'll be warned. Large PDFs are the usual cause. Share large plans via a file service and reference them in the scope notes instead.

Importing from a Spreadsheet

Tap 📊 Import beside + Create Job on the Estimates screen to import a MorForm Estimate Template (.xlsx). After the file is parsed, a job picker lets you choose which job to apply the data to. Labour rows, materials, subtrade amounts, and scope of work all populate automatically from the standard column format. You can also import directly from inside the Externals tab when a quote is already open — that version applies the import immediately to the open quote.

💡Download a blank MorForm Estimate Template from Settings → Data & Backup → Budget XLSX. Fill it in with your CSI line items and import it instead of entering rows manually.

Add change orders in the Estimates → Changes section. Each CO has:

FieldDescription
DateWhen the change was identified or submitted to the client
DescriptionWhat changed — be specific. Appears on the Internal Summary PDF.
AdjustmentPositive to add to contract, negative to deduct. Grand total updates live.
Client StatusPending / Approved / Rejected — tap to change
Approval DateShown when status is Approved — date client signed off
💡The net total of all approved change orders is broken out separately in the Summary so you always know your current contracted value vs. your original quote.

Client Invoicing

Emailing the Invoice Directly

In Estimates → Info, enter the client's email address. On the Summary screen the Share Client Invoice button becomes 📧 Email Invoice to [Client Name]. Tapping it generates the PDF and opens a pre-addressed email with it attached in one tap.

Tracking Submission & Approval

Set the status to Submitted and enter the date once you've sent the quote. When the client approves, set to Approved and enter the approval date. If they provide a PO number, enter it in the Client PO field. All three print on the Internal Summary PDF.

ℹ️If the client email is not set, the button shows Share Client Invoice which opens the share sheet — you can still email it manually from there.

Crew Directory

Tap + Add to add a supervisor manually. Supervisors are also added automatically the first time they send you a .morrep report — their name, email, and phone come from their MorForm profile.

CardShows

Inbox — Reports

When a supervisor sends a job report, tap the .morrep attachment in your email — MorForm Manager opens and imports it automatically. Or tap Import in the Inbox tab to pick the file manually.

ℹ️Reports are sent per job — not per pay period. When a supervisor taps 📤 Send on a job card, it sends all time, expenses, and safety docs for that specific job. You may receive multiple reports per supervisor if they work across several jobs.

What's in a Report

DataDetails
Time entriesEvery hour by date and job site, with OT and stat holiday flags
ExpensesAll amounts by category with reimbursable total
Safety summaryCount of each form type — not the actual PDFs
Incident reportsFull incident details included when present — triggers the sign-off workflow
Job statusesCurrent status of dispatched jobs — your pipeline updates on approval
Trade scope completionsWhich trades have had their scope marked complete by the supervisor
Unmatched jobsHours on jobs not dispatched from your app — flagged but still visible

Incident Report Sign-off

🚨When a report contains an incident report, a red 🚨 INCIDENT badge appears on the report card. Alberta OH&S requires the employer to investigate and document corrective actions. The sign-off in MorForm creates this record.

Open the report — a dedicated red sign-off section appears below the safety documents. For each incident:

1
Review the incident description
The supervisor's incident details, date, location, and notes are shown.
2
Enter corrective actions
Describe what was done, root cause, who is responsible for follow-up, and by when.
3
Tap ✅ Sign Off
Records your name, timestamp, and notes permanently. This is your investigation record — it can be provided to an OHS officer on request.
ℹ️Incident sign-off is separate from report approval. Sign off the incident first, then approve the full report. Sign-off cannot be undone.

Approving a Report

1
Review the report
Check hours by job, expense amounts, and any unmatched job warnings.
2
Sign off any incidents first
If a red 🚨 section appears, complete the incident sign-off before approving.
3
Tap ✓ Approve & Commit
Hours and expenses commit to job actuals. Job statuses update. Supervisor is auto-added to Crew directory if new. Report is marked reviewed.
🚨Approval cannot be undone. Hours and expenses lock into the job cost record permanently. Review carefully before approving — especially check for unmatched job IDs and unusually large expenses.

Reports & Actuals

Tap 📊 Reports in the header. Set a date range — defaults to the current month. Three views:

ViewShows
SummaryTotal hours, OT hours, total expenses, reimbursable total, safety doc count, number of supervisors and submissions for the date range
By JobHours and expenses rolled up per job site. OT shown separately. For billing and profitability.
By SupervisorHours, OT, expenses, reimbursable amounts, and report count per supervisor. For payroll review.

Tap the CSV button on any view to download a spreadsheet of the filtered data.

💡The budget progress bar on each job detail updates automatically each time you approve a supervisor report. Check it regularly — it turns orange at 80% and red at 100% of the estimated budget.
📝
Notes

Internal reminders visible only to you. Tap + Add Note, write your note, optionally link it to a job and/or supervisor, and save. Tap a note to mark it done or delete it.

💡Good uses: permit application follow-ups, client call reminders, delegation notes, inspection schedules, outstanding change order approvals — anything that doesn't fit elsewhere.

How Both Apps Connect

MorForm Manager and MorForm (the supervisor app) are completely independent. No shared server, no accounts, no subscriptions. All data moves through regular email attachments using three custom file types.

FileDirectionContains
.morjobYou → SupervisorJob, dates, scope, PM contact, all contacts, trade contacts, permits, photos, documents
.morrepSupervisor → YouTime entries, expenses, safety doc summaries, job statuses, trade scope completions
.mordataManager ↔ OfficeFull data snapshot — jobs, estimates, budgets, PMs, crew, notes. Used for Office handoff or desktop continuation.

Other emails supervisors send

WhatGoes to
Safety form PDFs (FLHA, ERP, Inspection)Their Safety Manager
Incident Report PDFsTheir Safety Manager and you (Project Manager) — triggers sign-off workflow in your app
Timesheet PDF + CSVTheir Manager/Supervisor email
Expense report PDF + CSVTheir Accounting email
Progress photos + notesYou (Project Manager)
Interactive blank formsSubtrades (who submit back to supervisor + safety manager)
ℹ️Your supervisor's .morrep report includes safety doc counts (FLHA: 10, Inspection: 2, etc.) but not the actual PDF forms. Safety PDFs go directly to the Safety Manager — you see compliance numbers, not form content.

Send to Office / Handoff

MorForm Manager can export your full data snapshot and send it to MorForm Office on your desktop, or hand off a project to another project manager.

1
Open Settings → Data & Backup
2
Tap 🖥 Send to Office / Handoff
Your own email is selected by default. Tap "Choose PMs" to also send to project managers from your PM list.
3
Mail app opens pre-addressed
Share the .mordata attachment from the share sheet.
4
Recipient imports the file
On MorForm Office: Settings → Data & Backup → 📱 Import .mordata. On another Manager mobile: tap the attachment directly.
💡The .mordata file contains all jobs, estimates, budgets, PMs, crew, and notes. Choose Merge to add new items alongside existing data, or Replace All to fully swap — double confirmed before proceeding.

Import from Office or Another Manager

Tap 📦 Import from Manager / Office in Settings → Data & Backup, or tap a .mordata email attachment directly. You'll be offered Merge or Replace All.

⚠️Factory Reset permanently deletes all data — all jobs, estimates, crew history, and submissions. Back up your data first using the Back Up App Data button in Data & Backup.
