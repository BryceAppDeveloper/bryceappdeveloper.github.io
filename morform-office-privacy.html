Privacy Policy

MorForm Office

Effective date: June 6, 2026  ·  Developer: Bryce Duhamel
🖥 Windows Desktop Application
MorForm Office is a Windows desktop application for construction project managers. It does not send, transmit, or sell your personal data to any server, third party, or analytics platform. The information you enter — jobs, estimates, contacts, and imported reports — is stored only on your computer, and is shared only when you explicitly choose to send it — by email, or through a shared cloud folder you connect (see Section 3). No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs are used; the app is built with Electron, which transmits no personal data.

1. Who We Are

MorForm Office is developed and maintained by Bryce Duhamel, based in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. For privacy inquiries, contact: privacy@morform.app

2. What Data the App Stores

MorForm Office stores the following information locally on your Windows computer only:

  • Your name, job title, email address, and phone number (entered during setup)
  • Company information — name, address, phone, website, and logo image
  • Job records — names, addresses, scope notes, status, contacts, permits, and associated photos and documents
  • Estimates — CSI division rows, labour, material costs, subcontractor costs, markup, and scope of work
  • Crew directory — project managers, supervisors, safety officers, employees, and contacts you add manually
  • Vendor and subcontractor contact directory
  • Supervisor field reports imported from .morrep files — time entries, expenses, and safety form data
  • PDF quote settings — quote number counter, terms text, and signature image
  • Report routing email addresses for accounting and safety forwarding

All data is stored in a single JSON file at %APPDATA%\morform-office\morform-office-data.json on your local machine. The developer operates no server, so none of this data is transmitted to the developer; it leaves your computer only when you choose to share it (see Section 3).

3. How Data Leaves Your Computer

Data only leaves your computer when you explicitly choose to dispatch a job, approve a supervisor report, or export a file. MorForm Office communicates through your existing email client or, optionally, a shared cloud folder you connect:

Outgoing (.morjob files) When you dispatch a job to a supervisor, MorForm Office packages the job details, contacts, permits, scope notes, photos, and documents into a .morjob file and opens your default email client. You control the recipient and whether to send.
Incoming (.morrep files) Supervisor field reports arrive as .morrep email attachments. You save the file to your computer and import it manually. MorForm Office reads the file locally — no data is sent back automatically.
Report routing (on approval) When you approve a supervisor report, MorForm Office can open your email client with a pre-composed expense or safety summary message addressed to your accounting or safety email. You control whether to send.
Exported files XLSX estimates, PDF quotes, and JSON backups are saved to your local file system. You control if and how you share them.
Optional Company Cloud Folder sync Instead of (or in addition to) email, you can connect a shared folder so the same .morjob / .morrep / .mordata files exchange automatically with your crew. MorForm Office reads and writes those files in an ordinary folder on your PC — one that your own cloud provider's app (Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive or similar) keeps synced. When you enable this, the files are stored in and synced through that folder under your cloud provider's terms and privacy policy. MorForm operates no server and never receives your cloud account or password. Nothing syncs to any cloud unless you set this up.

Aside from a cloud folder you choose to connect, MorForm Office operates no server, API, or cloud storage of its own, and performs no background transmission to the developer.

4. Permissions and System Access

MorForm Office requests the following system access:

  • File system (read/write) — to read imported files (.morjob, .morrep, XLSX, CSV, images) and save exports, backups, and the local data file
  • Default email client — to open pre-addressed compose windows when dispatching jobs or routing reports. MorForm Office does not send email directly; it opens your email client with a pre-filled message that you review and send
  • Open file dialog — to let you browse for files to import
  • Save file dialog — to let you choose where to save exports

No microphone, camera, location, notification, or network permissions are requested or used.

5. Supervisor Report Data

When you import a .morrep file from a supervisor, it may contain that supervisor's personal information including their name, email address, time records, expense data, and safety form submissions. This information is:

  • Stored locally on your computer only
  • Used solely for project management and payroll reference purposes
  • Never transmitted to any third party or external server
  • Deletable at any time by clearing the app data file or uninstalling the app

6. Third-Party Services

MorForm Office does not integrate with any analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting services, telemetry services, or social media SDKs. The application is built on Electron (Node.js) and uses only standard system APIs.

The XLSX library (SheetJS) used for spreadsheet import and export runs entirely locally and does not make any network requests.

When you send a dispatch or export via email, it travels through your own email account and provider — subject to your email provider's privacy policy.

7. Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored in your Windows user profile directory at %APPDATA%\morform-office\. This location is protected by your Windows user account permissions. The developer has no ability to access, view, or retrieve data stored on your computer.

You can export a full JSON backup from Settings → Data & Backup at any time, and restore it on any Windows machine running MorForm Office.

8. Microsoft Store

MorForm Office is distributed through the Microsoft Store. Microsoft's standard store telemetry and crash reporting may apply according to Microsoft's own privacy policy. MorForm Office itself does not add any additional telemetry beyond what the Microsoft Store platform collects at the OS level.

9. Children's Privacy

MorForm Office is a professional tool intended for adults working in the construction industry. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect or store information from minors.

10. Your Rights (PIPEDA — Canada)

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. Since all data is stored locally on your computer, you can delete it at any time by removing the data file at %APPDATA%\morform-office\morform-office-data.json or by uninstalling MorForm Office.

For any privacy concerns, contact privacy@morform.app.
