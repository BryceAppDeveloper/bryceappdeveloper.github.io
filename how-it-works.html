How it works

No server in the middle —
just files you control

MorForm's three apps exchange jobs, reports and data as small files. They move by email, or through a shared folder you connect in your company's own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive. MorForm runs no servers of its own — either way the files stay in your accounts and on your devices.

The loop

One job, three apps, zero re-entry

A job starts in the office, gets dispatched to the field, and the field report flows back — each handoff is a single, standard file. No portal logins, no copy-paste between systems.

The file types

Three small files do all the work

.morjob — the dispatch
  • Manager or Office → Supervisor
  • Carries job spec, scope, trades, plans, permits and contacts
  • Tap the attachment to import the whole job in one step
.morrep — the field report
  • Supervisor → Manager or Office
  • Carries hours, expenses, completed scope and safety forms
  • Safety PDFs route to the right inbox automatically
.mordata — the full sync
  • Manager ↔ Office
  • A full snapshot — jobs, estimates, crew and actuals
  • Keeps the office desktop and the field manager in step

Two ways to move the files

Pick whichever fits your crew. Both move the exact same files — there's no MorForm server in either path.

📧
By email
Managers dispatch jobs as .morjob attachments, supervisors send back .morrep reports, and Office syncs with Manager via .mordata. Works with any email you already use.
☁️
By your own cloud folder
Connect a shared Company Cloud Folder in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive. The apps read and write the same files there automatically — MorForm never sees your cloud login.
