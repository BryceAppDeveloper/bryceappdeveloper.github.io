How it works

No server in the middle: just files you control

MorForm has no accounts and no servers. The three apps pass work between each other as small files that move by email, or through a shared folder in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive. Either way, the files only ever live in accounts you own.

The loop

One job, three apps, zero re-entry

A job is estimated at the desk, dispatched to the field, worked and documented on site, and reported back. Each handoff is one file.

MorForm OfficeThe estimating desk · Windows
  • Builds the estimate: divisions, line items, markup, client quote PDF
  • Won estimates become jobs with the budget carried over
  • Dispatches the job package to a supervisor as a .morjob file
.mordata ⇄.morjob ↓
MorForm ManagerThe PM's site tool · iOS & Android
  • Specs the job: type, subtypes, trades and scope from a saved library
  • Sends job requests to Office as .mordata; dispatches .morjob packages to supervisors
  • Receives .morrep reports; approving them locks hours and expenses to actuals
.morjob ↓↑ .morrep
MorForm SupervisorThe crew's logbook · iOS & Android
  • Imports the .morjob: contacts, permits, scope land intact, nothing re-typed
  • Logs hours and expenses, completes FLHA and safety forms on site
  • Sends everything home in one .morrep file: timesheet, receipts, signed safety PDFs

The file formats

Three small files carry everything

Every MorForm file is plain data you can see, keep, forward, and back up. No file ever has to touch a MorForm server. There isn't one.

.morjob: a dispatched job

Manager / Office → Supervisor
  • Job info, site address and access notes
  • Client and vendor contacts
  • Permits, plans, photos and scope

.morrep: a field report

Supervisor → Manager / Office
  • Pay-period timesheet with OT and stat flags
  • Expenses with receipt photos attached
  • Signed FLHA, inspections and safety PDFs

.mordata: a full handoff

Manager ⇄ Office
  • Job specs sent to Office become estimates
  • Estimates and budgets flow back to the PM
  • Full snapshots for backup and migration

Two ways to move files

By email, or by a cloud folder you own

Both work offline-first: everything is created on the device, then moves when you're back in range. Pick one, or use both.

Option 1 · Email

Attach and send, nothing to set up

Dispatching a job saves a .morjob and opens your email with it attached. Supervisors send .morrep reports back the same way. Works with any email account, from day one.

Option 2 · Company Cloud Folder

A shared folder in your own cloud

Connect a folder in your own Google Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive. Dispatches and reports drop in automatically and every connected device picks them up. MorForm never sees your cloud login. The folder, and everything in it, is yours.

Either way: no MorForm account, and no MorForm server. If MorForm disappeared tomorrow, your files would still be sitting in your own email and your own cloud folder.
